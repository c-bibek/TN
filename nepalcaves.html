Nepals Caves becoming popular among Tourists


According to the Kathmandu based English daily, The Himalayan Times, 1,250metres long Manjushree cave( probably the second-longest cave in Asia) located at Chovar, Kathmandu is becoming popuar among tourists. The cave is located near Chovar gorge, which according to local legend ,was created by Boddhisattva's Manjushree sword. Only 342 metres of the 1250 metres long cave has been opened to visitors. Siddhagufa cave at Bimalnagar in Bandipur is also proving popular among tourists.
